Why Custom?

Not Everyone Fits a Standard Programme — and That's Perfectly Fine

Our other services address specific, well-defined health goals. But many of our clients come to us with situations that are more complex — or more unique — than a single-focus programme can address.

Perhaps you have three health conditions, each with different dietary requirements. Perhaps your cultural and religious eating restrictions are highly specific. Perhaps you've tried every standard approach and nothing has worked. Perhaps your goal is unusual enough that no existing programme addresses it.

"Every human being is unique. Their diet plan should be too. We've never created two identical custom plans — not once in ten years."

The Custom Diet Plan starts with a 60-minute deep-dive assessment — the most comprehensive intake we offer. We leave no stone unturned. The result is a plan that fits you so precisely that it stops feeling like a diet and starts feeling like just the way you eat.

Who Is This For?

You Need a Custom Plan If…

You have multiple health conditions

E.g. PCOS + hypothyroid + weight gain, or diabetes + hypertension + kidney disease. Standard programmes can't balance these safely.

Your diet has strict restrictions

Jain (no root veg, no eating after sunset), strict vegan, Sattvic (no onion/garlic), raw food, or very specific religious fasting requirements.

You're planning for a child or elderly person

Paediatric nutrition, senior citizen diets, or eating plans during pregnancy require highly specialised approaches.

Your cultural cuisine is unusual

NRIs with access only to Western ingredients, regional Indian cuisines with very specific ingredients, or cross-cultural cooking situations.

Your goal is highly unique

E.g. preparing for Ironman while managing hypothyroid, or building muscle on a Jain diet at 65 — no standard service covers this.

Standard programmes haven't worked

If you've tried multiple diets and nothing has worked long-term, a fully bespoke assessment often reveals the specific pattern that was missing.

Our Deep-Dive Process

How We Build Your Custom Plan

Our custom plan process is the most thorough assessment we offer — because your situation deserves more than a quick questionnaire.

01
📋

60-Minute Deep-Dive Assessment

A comprehensive video or in-person session covering your complete medical history, all diagnoses, current medications, blood reports, food allergies, cultural dietary restrictions, eating schedule, cooking habits, lifestyle, stress, sleep, and goals.

02
🔬

Medical & Nutritional Analysis

We review all your blood reports and medical documents. If reports are missing, we recommend which ones to get. We identify nutritional deficiencies, metabolic patterns, and condition-specific dietary requirements that must be balanced.

03
🍽️

Bespoke Plan Construction

Your plan is built from scratch — not adapted from a template. Every meal, every food choice, every timing recommendation is determined by your specific data. This typically takes 24–48 hours to complete with the depth it deserves.

04
📱

Plan Walkthrough Session

A follow-up call to walk through your plan in detail — explaining why each food is included, how to prepare it, what to substitute if needed, and how to apply it to your real daily life.

05
🔄

Weekly Reviews & Adjustments

Your plan is reviewed every week based on your feedback, blood sugar readings, weight changes, or any symptom updates. Nothing is static — your plan evolves as you do.

06
📅

Quarterly Full Revisions

Every 3 months, we conduct a full reassessment — reviewing updated blood reports, body composition changes, and evolving goals — and rebuild your plan for the next phase.
