Myth vs. Fact

Common Weight Loss Myths — Busted

MythYou must avoid carbs completely to lose weight.
FactCarbs are your body's primary energy source. The right type and timing of carbs actually speeds up fat loss.
MythEating less is always the answer.
FactUnder-eating slows your metabolism. Strategic calorie distribution is far more effective than just eating less.
MythYou need a gym membership to lose weight.
FactNutrition drives 70–80% of weight loss results. Exercise accelerates progress but is not a prerequisite.
MythWeight loss supplements accelerate results.
FactNo supplement replaces balanced nutrition. Most do nothing — some are actively harmful.
MythIndian food is too heavy to eat on a weight loss diet.
FactIndian cuisine is naturally rich in protein, fibre, and complex carbs. The right portions make it ideal for weight loss.
