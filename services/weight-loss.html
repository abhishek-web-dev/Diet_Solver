Myth vs. Fact

Common Weight Loss Myths — Busted

Understanding the truth behind weight loss is the first step toward a successful transformation. Here are the most common misconceptions we clear for our clients.

MythYou must avoid carbs completely to lose weight.
FactCarbs are your body's energy source. Right timing speeds up fat loss.
MythEating less is always the answer.
FactUnder-eating slows metabolism. Strategic distribution is better.
MythYou need a gym membership to lose weight.
FactNutrition drives 70–80% of results. Exercise is an accelerator.
MythWeight loss supplements accelerate results.
FactNo supplement replaces balanced nutrition. Most do nothing.
MythIndian food is too heavy for weight loss.
FactIndian cuisine is rich in fibre and complex carbs. Portions matter.
