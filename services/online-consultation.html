Why Online Works

The Same Expertise — From the Comfort of Your Home

When we opened our online consultation service, we made one non-negotiable promise: online clients receive exactly the same quality of care as those who visit our Delhi clinic in person. No shortcuts, no reduced assessments, no templated plans.

Everything that happens in an in-person session — the detailed health intake, the review of blood reports, the personalised plan design, the follow-up sessions, the WhatsApp support — happens identically online. The only difference is that you don't have to travel.

Our online sessions are available across all Indian time zones and for international clients. Morning, afternoon, and evening slots are available on weekdays, with Saturday slots available for working professionals.

Dr. Aisha will admit you from the waiting room promptly at your time.
