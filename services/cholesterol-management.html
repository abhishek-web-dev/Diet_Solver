Lipid Profile 101

Understanding Your Numbers

LDL (Bad Cholesterol)

This builds up in your arteries, forming plaques that can lead to heart disease. Our goal is to lower this through fibre and healthy fats.

HDL (Good Cholesterol)

This acts like a vacuum cleaner, carrying LDL away from the arteries and back to the liver. We aim to raise this through exercise and quality fats.

How We Do It

Heart-Healthy Diet Strategies

Solble Fibre Focus

Oats, barley, and beans contain soluble fibre that binds to cholesterol in the digestive system and drags it out of the body.

The Right Fats

Replacing saturated fats (butter, ghee) with healthy unsaturated fats (nuts, seeds, mustard oil) significantly improves lipid profiles.

Plant Sterols

Naturally occurring plant compounds that block the absorption of cholesterol. We include these through seeds and vegetables.
